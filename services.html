Mixing & Mastering Services

A great recording starts with great performances. From there, a good tracking engineer will get you 80% of the way, but that final 20% can make or break it. Without a good mix, your songs won't live up to their full potential, and from there, proper mastering adds the polish that will make your songs ready to present to the world.

I treat music with the respect it deserves. When mixing or mastering, I constantly ask myself, "is what I am doing enhancing the music or detracting from it?" My ultimate goal is bring out the full potential of the music, and I am not happy unless my clients are happy.

Session Drummer

I am available as a drummer for sessions & live shows. For the budget conscious or those without access to recording facilities, I offer Online Session Services as well.

Location Recording

Got a cool space you'd like to record in but don't have the gear or an engineer to run it? No worries, I can come to you. I can also record live performances, rehearsals, foley/sound effects, voiceovers, etc. on location.

Recent Projects

Dread Fascination

Dread Fascination is my free-to-listen debut album. It represents a unique approach to concept albums, with no characters and no dialogue; Instead, it attempts to tell a story through different perspectives, bringing to light the thoughts & emotions that come about from experiencing tragic or life-altering events. It explores themes of love, loss, depression, death & rebirth, and the process of finding purpose and fulfillment in the face of life's resistance.
