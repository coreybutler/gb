Dread Fascination

Dread Fascination is an upcoming free-to-listen album by musician Graham Butler. It represents a unique approach to concept albums, with no characters and no dialogue; Instead, it attempts to tell a story simply through the thoughts, emotions, and words that come about from experiencing tragic or life-altering events. It explores the process of finding purpose and fulfillment in the face of life's resistance.

Coming early Fall 2013.
