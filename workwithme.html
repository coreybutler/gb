How it Works

If you would like to work with me on your project, please Contact me with the following information:

Mixing or Mastering:

  • Project type: Mixing, Mastering, or both; single track or group of tracks, EP, full-length record, commercial spot, voiceover, etc.
  • Band/Artist name (if applicable)
  • Music style or genre
  • Approximate length of all material
  • Deadline or timeframe for completion
  • Approximate budget

Custom Drum Tracks or Online Sessions:

  • Band/Artist name (if applicable)
  • Music style or genre
  • Number of Songs
  • Deadline or timeframe for completion

Drummer for Sessions or Live Shows:

  • Band/Artist name (if applicable)
  • Music style or genre
  • Date(s) of sessions or performances
  • Approximate budget

On-location Recording:

  • Location of Recording project
  • Project type: Band, Singer/Songwriter, Live show recording, Foley/Sound Effects, etc.
  • Band/Artist name (if applicable)
  • Music style or genre (if applicable)
  • Number of Songs (if applicable)
  • Deadline or timeframe for completion

I will respond ASAP to discuss the project in further detail.
