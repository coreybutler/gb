A Little About Me

I am a professional live/session drummer based in Los Angeles, CA. In my decade of experience, I've worked with artists as diverse as Donald Brown (Jazz Messengers), Jeff Coffin (Dave Matthews Band), Wallace Coleman (Robert Lockwood Jr.), Kirk Whalum (Whitney Houston), Peter Calo (Carly Simon), Kenny Vaughan (Marty Stewart), Michael Webb (LeAnn Rimes), Dave Jacques (John Prine) and many others.

In 2003 I began studying the arts of recording and production, and kicked my studies into overdrive in 2006 when I began the recording of my debut solo album, Dread Fascination. From 2003 until now, I've recorded and produced music for a variety of clients including bands, solo artists and students. Since 2012 I have been offering mixing & mastering services both locally in Southern CA and internationally through the web.

I am currently available as a drummer/musical director for sessions and touring, as an engineer for on-location tracking and mixing, or as a producer for bands and other music projects.

If you are interested in working with me, feel free to contact me and tell me about your project!

  • Recent Projects

    Dread Fascination is my free-to-listen debut album. It represents a unique approach to concept albums, with no characters and no dialogue; Instead, it attempts to tell a story through different perspectives, bringing to light the thoughts & emotions that come about from experiencing tragic or life-altering events. It explores themes of love, loss, depression, death & rebirth, and the process of finding purpose and fulfillment in the face of life's resistance.
