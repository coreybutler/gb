Custom Drum Tracks

A great recording starts with great performances, and one of the most important (and most often overlooked) aspects of a recording is the drum tracks. A great drum track provides the feel and power (or sensitivity) that drives a song.

Programmed drums are sounding better than ever, but they still cannot provide the depth of feel and the touch a real drummer is capable of. Great drummers are few and far between, and if you do manage to find one, getting him/her into a studio to record can be an expensive and time-consuming proposition.

That's where I come in. I have a studio full of drums ready to record, and you can take advantage of my experience and expertise as a drummer and recording engineer to create drum tracks that are custom-tailored to your songs. Here are some reasons to choose me:

  • Detail is everything:
    • I believe that the right drum part and the right sound is key to bringing out the best in a song. I will develop a drum part that serves the emotion and style of your song, taking the following into account:
      1. Drum and cymbal selection
      2. Drum tuning and cymbal pitch-matching
      3. Microphone selection and positioning
      4. Mic Pre/EQ/Compression chain
  • Expert recording techniques:

    In addition to drumming, I have worked as an engineer and producer for over ten years in my own studio and others. You can rest assured you will get tracks recorded at optimal levels with no undesireable clipping, distortion, or artifacts; Guaranteed.

  • Options, options, options:

    I have a wide variety of drums, cymbals and percussion instruments to work with, so you can count on getting the right sounds.

  • Extras:

    For each song, you have additional options:

    1. Beat Detective / Time Alignment: If your music is strongly quantized to a grid, I can do the work of time-aligning the drum part to match perfectly with your sequenced tracks.
    2. Sampled Hits: You can choose to have soft, medium, and loud hits on each drum and cymbal, recorded separately, to use for sample replacement or edits. You are also free to use the samples in later projects.
    3. Loop Construction: If your arrangement is not yet finalized, I can create drum loops that you can use to build it up.

  • Mixing & Mastering

    Got all your tracks recorded but aren't able to get the project finalized? Take advantage of my Mixing & Mastering services to take your project to the next level.

  • Satisfaction Guaranteed or you don't pay:

    I understand how important your music is to you. Because of this, I will do up to two additional takes for every song, free of charge. If you still aren't happy with the results, you don't pay a cent. Guaranteed.
